Origami


I first came across Origami in 2010 when I was bored at my friends house. He had a multitude of drawers in his room, one of which was labeled origami. I asked him to teach me something and he explained the process for folding a crane. I was immediately enraptured by the transition from a plain and simple square into a complex figure. I continued to buy all the origami books I could and fold every model I came across. That summer I went to the Origami USA Convention with my friend and was introduced to the origami community. It was an incredibly enjoyable experience and when I my interest in origami was resparked in 2014 I went to the convention once more. I continue to fold today and origami continues to amaze me with its complexity, beauty, and mathematical basis.
